About Us

Hi, we are... EcoLifeHub

At EcoLifeHub we believe that creating a better future for us and the next generation starts with living a sustainable life. We created this website with the intention of raising awareness. To do this the website gives users information and resources, whilst inspiring them to take action throughout their life.

The definition of sustainable according to the dictionary is ‘conserving an ecological balance by avoiding depletion of natural resources’. To be sustainable we have to find the balance between, economic prosperity, environmental preservation and social equity. By being sustainable we are able to notice how our actions throughout the day effects our resources, Earth and everyone around us.

Our Aproach

Our platforms goal is to educate users into learning areas of sustainability including; fashion, travel; ethical consumption; and even energy conservation. We aim to inform and empower people by creating a community of individual dedicated to helping the world move into a sustainable direction, showing that we have the ability to make a change.

We offer success stories, tips, guidance and innovative initiatives. Our articles located on the news page, is a great way to start learning more. Let’s create a better future for our children, and give them the chance to see the earth flourish.
